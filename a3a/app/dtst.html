Entered occasion testing mode.

The format to test dates is the app's full URL with #date?DAYxMONTH added to the end, for example: niconicosette . github . io/i/a3a/app/#date?31x6 will change the date to June 31st (you'd have to remove the spaces around the periods ofc).

When testing, please test all characters including side characters (side characters have holiday voices and player birthday voices, but no cast birthday voices). Also, please confirm that Act 1 cards with (1) in the title have different voices and messages from Act 2 cards with (2) in the title. For example, an Act (1) Misumi card should have a different voice than an Act (2) Misumi card.

To test a new date, changing the URL and refreshing won't work. You'll have to close the window and then enter a new date specifying URL to visit.
